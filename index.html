About Me

I have been interested in and have practiced many artistic talents ever since I was little. I am an avid character/concept designer, animator, artist, graphic designer, writer, and entrepeneur. I have won first place in the Arizona State Fair for 2D art many times, and I enjoy mediums such as Acrylic, gouache, watercolor, soft pastels, and both 2D and 3D animation. I have also previously sold my designs online, and will be attending my first convention in January 2023.
